So, what am I supposed to do?

You are supposed to use your phone to collect accelerometer data while you perform an activity.

How do I start logging?

  1. Press on a label to start the logger.
  2. Put your phone into your pocket:
    • LEFT POCKET
    • UPSIDE DOWN
    • SCREEN FACING LEG
  3. Perform the activity.
  4. Stop the logger when done or select another activity.

How do I stop logging?

  • Press STOP LOGGING on the action bar.
  • Press the red activity label again.

How can I submit my logs?

  1. Switch to the Files tab.
  2. Long press on a log.
  3. Now you can select all logs from the action bar.
  4. Press the share button.
  5. Select Dropbox or email to send the logs
  6. After they were sent you can delete the logs from the list

You might want to wait 'til you're connected to a WLAN, otherwise sending the logs might eat up your precious data allowance.

I messed up a log, how can I delete it?

  1. Switch to the Files tab.
  2. Long press on a log to select it.
  3. Delete it (button on action bar).

How can I add a new label?

  1. Swith to the LOGGER tab.
  2. Press on the ADD LABEL action on the action bar
  3. Insert the name of the activity.
  5. Press save.
